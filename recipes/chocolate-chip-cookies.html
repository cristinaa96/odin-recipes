Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description of Dish

A classic cookie recipe that you need to make at least once in your life! Enjoy with a nice glass of milk (or any drink of your choice) to get the full experience.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown. The oven should be preheated to 350 degrees F and it should take about 10 minutes.
